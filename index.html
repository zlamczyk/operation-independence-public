How scoring works
  • Regular post: 1 post point + 2 coffeeteria points = 3 points
  • Q: 5 Q points + 2 coffeeteria points; Qs do not also receive the normal post point
  • Downrange post: 10 points + 2 coffeeteria points
  • Downrange Q: 10 Downrange post points + 20 Downrange Q points + 2 coffeeteria points
  • Different established Ironplains AO after your first: +5 points each
  • Ironplains Passport, post at every listed Ironplains AO: 25 points
  • Traveler Bonus, post at 5 different AOs outside your normal AO: 25 points
  • Shield Lock: 4-7 attended/Qed beatdowns = 10 points; 8+ = 25 points
  • Kotter Returns 30+ Days: automatically detected when possible.
  • VQ: automatically awarded when a PAX has his first recorded Q during July.
  • Passport, Traveler, Kotter, VQ, Shield Lock, and Lazarus are one-time bonuses. Manual claims include Bring FNG, Bring back visiting PAX, Service project participate, Service project organize, Brotherhood Builder, and Mentor a Brother.
